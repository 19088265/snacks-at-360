Welcome to our new mobile app, Snacks at 360. You can use it to buy items from the tuckshop, so that means no more jotting down in cursive and tallies. Sounds great, right? It's a very simple app, not rocket science.


The app is still under development, so further updates will include:

  • Adding your username when signing up
  • Setting a monthly limit for your account
  • Making snack suggestions to the admin
  • Viewing the top 10 monthly customers of the tuckshop (Yes, we wanna expose your financial choices).

Please feel free to give us any suggested features that you'd like to see on the app.
